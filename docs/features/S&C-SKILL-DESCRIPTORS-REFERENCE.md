## 1Ô∏è‚É£ RUNNING & ENDURANCE

### Skill 1: Aerobic Capacity
**What:** Ability to sustain prolonged aerobic effort (VO2max, endurance base)

| Rating | Descriptor |
|--------|------------|
| **1.0** | Struggles with continuous 10+ minute efforts |
| **2.0** | Can sustain moderate effort for 20-30 minutes |
| **3.0** | Can sustain effort for 40-60 minutes at steady pace |
| **4.0** | Maintains high work rate for 60+ minutes with minimal decline |
| **5.0** | Exceptional endurance, can sustain near-maximal effort for extended periods |

**Test:** Beep test, 12-min run, time trial, match observation

---

### Skill 2: Running Economy
**What:** Efficiency of running gait, energy cost per km

| Rating | Descriptor |
|--------|------------|
| **1.0** | Inefficient gait, high energy cost, poor mechanics |
| **2.0** | Developing technique, some wasted movement |
| **3.0** | Competent running mechanics, adequate efficiency |
| **4.0** | Efficient gait, minimal wasted energy, good form under fatigue |
| **5.0** | Exceptional running economy, maintains perfect form throughout |

**Test:** Video analysis, coach observation during runs

---

### Skill 3: Pacing Strategy
**What:** Ability to manage effort distribution across race/match duration

| Rating | Descriptor |
|--------|------------|
| **1.0** | Poor pacing, starts too fast or too slow, inconsistent |
| **2.0** | Basic pacing awareness, significant variation in splits |
| **3.0** | Can maintain relatively consistent pace in training |
| **4.0** | Good pace judgment, minimal variation, adapts to conditions |
| **5.0** | Exceptional pacing control, optimal energy distribution, competitive awareness |

**Test:** Time trials with splits, race analysis

---

### Skill 4: Recovery Between Efforts
**What:** Ability to recover between high-intensity bouts (Hyrox stations, GAA surges)

| Rating | Descriptor |
|--------|------------|
| **1.0** | Slow recovery, struggles to resume activity quickly |
| **2.0** | Moderate recovery, needs extended rest between efforts |
| **3.0** | Can recover within 60-90 seconds for next bout |
| **4.0** | Fast recovery, ready for next effort within 30-60 seconds |
| **5.0** | Exceptional recovery, minimal performance drop between bouts |

**Test:** Repeated sprint test with HR recovery, station simulations

## üìã RATING CHECKLIST

Before submitting each rating, ask yourself:

- ‚òê Have I observed this skill in **3+ different sessions**?
- ‚òê Have I seen it performed **fresh AND fatigued**?
- ‚òê Have I compared to the **descriptor** (not other athletes)?
- ‚òê Am I rating **current ability** (not potential or past)?
- ‚òê Would I defend this rating to **another coach**?
- ‚òê Have I documented **context and notes**?

If you answer NO to any question, observe more before rating.
